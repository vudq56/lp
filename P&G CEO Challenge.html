P&G CEO CHALLENGE?

Welcome you to the P&G CEO Challenge: A competition which will test your aptitude for business strategy and real-life problem-solving. If you’ve been eager to demonstrate your ingenuity and intelligence beyond your campus, this is the chance to make an impact on a global scale.


Prepare to compete with teams from around the world to see who is fittest to be the next CEO of P&G. Top students will ascend to the Global Final in Dubai and win the opportunity to meet P&G CEO David Taylor. First, prepare to showcase your ability to think critically about leading brand Gillette. Best of luck!

AN ADVENTURE WITH P&G CEO CHALLENGE

CV Screening & Interview: Submit your application by October 25th, 2018.
Complete your Tests (online and written) and Interview with P&G.
Vietnam Local Round
(21/11/2018 - 23/11/2018)
Build your capability by learning from your peers, receiving coaching from P&G Mentors, and competing in teams with other best students. Enjoy a trip in HCMC fully funded by P&G.
 Singapore Final Round
(13/2/2019 - 14/2/2019)
Represent P&G Vietnam to fly to P&G ASIA Headquarter, meet P&G Regional Executives and compete with best teams from 8 other countries. Show us your daring to be the best in Asia!
Global Final
(May 2019)
The winner from the Asia final will then fly to Dubai City to compete against finalists from all over the world. Present your solution and claim your invitation to the P&G 2018 Signal conference in Cincinnati.
